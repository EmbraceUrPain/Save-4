Gameplay

Classes

The game offers 9 unique classes at the start of the game. All classes have the Dodge and Break techniques. While the player cannot change their class after picking, all classes have similar stats and level up the same, with the exception of The Fluffy. Each class has a different appearance, though this can be changed in the Reminiscence Room located in the Library Dream after obtaining an ending.

【The Knight】 : "High Attack and Defense. Knows techniques for lowering enemy Attack and Defense." - Begin with the Knight's Sword, Knight's Shield, Knight's Helmet, and Knight's Armor.
【The Thief】 : "Incredibly high Speed. Knows a technique for slaying foes in a single blow. Begins with 1000 bonus Souls." - Begin with an extra 1000 Souls. Given the Thief's Dagger, Buckler, Thief's Mask, and Cloak of Shadows.
【The Sorcerer】 : "Incredibly high Magic. Knows how to cast Soul Arrow(attack sorcery)." - Given the Sorcerer's Staff, Round Shield, Magician Hat, Magician's Coat and Soul Arrow technique.
【The Cleric】 : "High Magic and Magic Defense. Knows how to cast Soul Light(healing sorcery)." - Given the Mace, Round Shield, Clergy Circlet, Clergy Clothes, and Soul Light technique.
【The Warrior】 : "Incredibly high Attack. His regular attacks inflict Break. Knows a technique that increases his Crit Chance." - Given the Club, Warrior's Round Shield, Viking Helm, and Warrior's Armor.
【The Templar】 : "High Attack, Defense, and Magic Defense. Knows how to cast Soul Arrow(attack sorcery) and can use a technique that lowers enemy Magic Defense." - Given the Halberd, Templar Shield, Helm of Milton, Armor of Milton, and Soul Arrow technique.
【The Hunter】 : "High Attack, Evasion, and Magic Evasion. Deals extra damage to beasts. Begins with a gun instead of a shield, which changes Break skill." - Given the Beasthunter Saw, Silver Pistol, and Hunter's Attire.
【The Explorer】 : "Incredibly high Luck. Has no particular strengths, but begins with a good number of consumables." - Begin with 5 Firebombs, 10 Blood Vials, 4 Blood Stanches, 4 Antidote Herbs, 2 Sedatives, 2 Pigeon's Eggs, 1 Homeward Bonedust, 1 Candy, 5 Girl's Photos. Given the Broken Sword, Wooden Shield, and Gentleman's Coat.
【The Fluffy】 : "A furry enigma. Begins with no equipment, but all of his parameters are slightly higher." - Begins with nothing. Starts with slightly higher stats. Levels up faster.

Souls

There are two types of souls - currency and stat enhancements. The first is used to increase the level of the character and all companions. Souls are obtained after killing an enemy or using the souls of dead people. To increase stats: Green, Purple, Red, Blue, Yellow, Ashen, Black, White and Tainted Black Souls. They can be found at locations or purchased.

Bonfire

Bonfires are used to restore health and mana, as well as for quick teleportation between locations. Unlike Dark Souls, bonfires do not save the player.

Companions

The player can also find companions in this game, although only in a temporary one-battle form, as opposed to the previous game. Most of the companions the player can encounter are Dream Spirits strewn about the lands of Wonderland. The player can recruit them before batlles with bosses by interacting with their books. Sometimes, for the books to appear, some othe conditions need to be met. The player also needs to not be Hollow, which is a state they get after every death. It can be cured with the Girl's Scent item.

SEN

A replacement of the Sin meter from the first game, SEN indicates the player's sanity or the lack of it. In the game, having it too low will cause you to see the inhabitants of Wonderland as monsters, and will have Nightmare Spirits more likely to attack you.

SEN is often more detrimental, the earlier you are into the game. However it is necessary to achieve all the endings, gain a lot of items and witness a lot of special interactions in Black Souls II.

It will cause you to see enemies that can not be beaten at earlier levels, be disabled from talking to some people or straight up kill them. It also allows to see memories from before...
